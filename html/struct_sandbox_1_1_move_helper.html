Detailed Description

This is the HL2 style movement. If moving from position using Velocity results in a collision velocity will be changed to slide across the surface where appropriate. Position will be updated to the optimal position.

This is coded to be simple on purpose. It's enough to get your started. Once you reach the point where it's lacking you should copy and paste it into your project and specialize to your needs.

Give it a position and velocity, set the Trace up how you want to use it, then you're good to go.
