Rotation Sandbox.InputMotionData.Rotation

Sensor-fused absolute rotation (will drift in heading)

The inertial measurement unit on the controller will create a quaternion based on fusing the gyro and the accelerometer. This value is the absolute orientation of the controller, but it will drift on the yaw axis.
