Detailed Description

A physically simulated spring. 'Length' is what's known as the 'natural spring length'. This is how long the spring would be if it was at rest (nothing hanging on it or attached). When you attach something to the spring, it will stretch longer than its 'natural length'. The amount of stretch is determined by the 'Spring Frequency'. The larger the spring frequency the less stretch the spring.
